Marketplace Whitepaper

In the year 3050

The world has evolved. Technology is the best we could have ever envisioned; This prosperity has come through great change. Nations no longer exist and the world is now ruled by clans. Disputes are no longer settled by conflict and war. Status, honor and wealth are now earned by racing in the Nitro League Arena.

Are you ready to race?

Features

  • Play to Earn

    Winning a match brings REP - reputation points, scales up the XP - experience level, & unlocks BLUEPRINTS to build cars.

  • NFT Upgrades

    Upgrade your vehicles to make them unique or faster in the race and increase their value in the marketplace.

  • Drive Tribe

    NFT owners can race on their own or sponsor other players with a DRIVE TRIBE scholarship - earning both the owner and the driver REP, XP and rewards.

  • Metaverse Linked

    Manage your profile as a racer, collector, shopkeeper, or any of the other roles available in the Nitro metaverse. Bring in your punk, vlfect, or ape andadd them as the skin on your car, display them in your garage or make it your identity!

  • New Rewards Mechanics

    Rewards can be gained in all game modes. All game rewards are ERC20 variable supply tokens, i.e. XP, REP & TICKETS.

  • DAOS, Tracks, Leagues

    NITRO ownership allows holders to create and join DAOs- create your own clan, design tracks, organise events, tournaments and more!

The Arena

The Arena is where legends are born. Drivers represent their clans, racing for victory, wealth and status as billions watch what unfolds. The fate of every clans’ future depends on what happens in the grand arena of the Nitro League

Experience a new era of racing.

Game Preview

Players can collect Nitro League cars, race them online, trade them on any NFT marketplace or showcase them across the metaverse. Take them into Terra Virtua, Decentraland or other metaverse spaces!

Choose your clan and enter the race to earn REP, XP and more. Upgrade your vehicle and brand it from avatars throughout the metaverse. Lock NITRO to turbocharge your progress and race to glory!

The Races

Nitro League hosts racing events both in game and within the community. Owners pay entry fees in NITRO to race their NFT cars against other players for prizes.

Let the race begin!
