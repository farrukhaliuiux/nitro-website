Team Nitro

Nitro League is being built by an amazing team with world-class credentials

  • Zaynab Tucker

    Chief Executive Officer

    A successful leader with 20+ years experience working across the technology, media and financial sectors. A history of driving digital transformation at Fortune 500 companies like HSBC, Mastercard, VISA and Emirates Group.

  • Glen Bullen

    Chief Operating Officer

    A highly-effective senior leader with over 20 years experience in start-up and growth phase companies, Glen delivers a wealth of expertise across all facets of the global iGaming and Digital Asset sectors and drives significant revenue and organizational growth via a strong operational, strategic, process-improvement and commercial focus.

    Chief Strategy Officer

    David is renowned in the Blockchain space for his contributions to notable projects, particularly regarding the definition of their token economy and strategy. Some of those projects include Holochain and Terra Virtua, both now on Binance.

    Chief Technology Officer

    Haseeb has been rated as one of the top technologists and chief software architects in the region. Having worked with the several start-ups as CTO, as well as delivered large scale enterprise and consumer platforms, Haseeb has delivered projects for companies including Yahoo!, AOL, Intuit, Groupon, ABB and WSJ.

  • Rizwan Siddiqui

    Chief Creative Officer

    Founding member of an award-winning gaming studio that has crossed 1M+ downloads/day. As a product designer and marketer, he has built digital products and campaigns for companies including NBC Universal, P&G, Barclays, Toyota and Manchester United.

  • Gasper Stih

    Chief Marketing Officer

    Gasper is on a mission to build brands that make an impact. He brought two ICO startups to life and played one of the key roles in one of the biggest fund investing campaigns that Europe saw in recent years. He’s a gamer, sportsman, crypto investor and loves cars (no surprise here).

  • Manuel Machado

    Chief Commercial Officer

    Manuel has +20 years international experience in Digital Transformation of large corporations and start-ups. For the last 5 years, his focus has been on Blockchain: as Global Head of Blockchain Solutions at Worldline, 4th largest payment service provider in the world; as an Expert Member of different think tanks (i.e. Alastria, EUBOF, INATBA or DEA); and as a Startup Advisor and Investor, with focus on NFT, Play-to-Earn, DAO and Tokenomics.

  • Sarah Da'na

    Marketing & Communications Specialist

    Sarah has international experience both in the large news and entertainment Media, such Al Jazeera and the Jordan Radio and Television Corporation, and in social media and influencer management. Sarah is a multifaceted professional taking critical responsibilities over Nitro League’s communications and community management.
